Proposals

These are design solutions for issues we have encountered within our cities. By using the observational and survey data, the projects below illustrate possible options that elevate specific sectors of the urban landscape. This exercise aims to provide examples for how a city can convert wasted space to be more efficient and improve other areas. Establishing a precedence for progress towards a smarter city.
