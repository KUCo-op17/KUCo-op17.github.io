A Tale of Four Cities

 

Studying these concepts is a challenging task, and no one city is a good analog for another. In order to better understand the question a whole, we must look to several places and aggregate our information about  them to synthesize our true parameters and means of making a difference.

 

A variety of data from different places will offer us insight into the essence of urbanism. As Current generations migrate back to cities, an urban fabric that can reinvent and enhance user experience is vital. Urban interventions respond to a city’s cultural identity, public spaces, and urban ecology via artists, designers, planners and members of the involved communities. Could we, as designers, take a step forward and imagine a Utopian future for our urban environments?
