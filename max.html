Chicago Design Proposal

Based on my research from articles, observations, and the data we' have collected, I want to focus my proposal on obsolete infrastructure. At the moment, I am exploring street parking as a component of the city that may soon be rendered unnecessary in a world where car ownership because a dated concept.

 

Our graphs show a that Chicago is very likely to use digital based means of transportation like Uber, and the graph is on the upward trend.

 

I also think that the work Andy Cohen has done with exploring the concept of a driver less world sets a projection that aligns with growing technology.
